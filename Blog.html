Welcome to Asphaltemia!

A Flexible Perspective from Grad School

Here, I'll weave together the threads of my academic endeavors, personal discoveries, and moments of revelation, all interwoven with the challenges and triumphs of my PhD journey. From delving into the mechanics of flexible pavements, their resiliency, and their role in modern transportation systems, to exploring the boundless potential of data science in revolutionizing civil engineering practices, this blog will serve as a beacon for those passionate about paving the way for a better, more connected world. Join me as we traverse the ever-evolving landscape of transportation engineering, uncovering hidden insights, demystifying complex concepts, and embracing the fusion of traditional wisdom with cutting-edge innovation. Whether you're a fellow scholar, a curious mind, or simply someone intrigued by the ways in which our world moves, I hope my experiences and the knowledge I share will inspire, enlighten, and spark conversations that propel us all toward a brighter future.
