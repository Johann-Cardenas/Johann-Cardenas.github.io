ICT: "Where Excellence and Transportation Meet"

I serve as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Illinois Center for Transportation (ICT) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. ICT works in partnership with the Illinois Department of Transportation (IDOT) to address the urgent and evolving transportation needs of the state, the country and the world.

CEE at Illinois

The CEE Department consistently ranks among the most elite programs in the U.S. and worldwide for both undergraduate and graduate studies. The Transportation Engineering group within CEE at Illinois offers unequalled expertise in pavement and transportation systems engineering.
