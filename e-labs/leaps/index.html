The engine

Multilayer elastic theory (Burmister) solved in Hankel-transform space — overflow-proof boundary conditions and near-exact surface responses.

Validation

A 69-check regression suite: Boussinesq closed forms, multilayer reduction, interface limits, equilibrium and Burmister factors. A subset runs at startup.

Conventions

Tension-positive stresses, z downward, deflection positive down. Uniform circular contacts; SI/US display, mm·N·MPa internally.
