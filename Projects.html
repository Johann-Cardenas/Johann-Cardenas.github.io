Research Projects

Advancing Transportation Engineering

The Illinois Center for Transportation (ICT) conducts research that goes from theoretical studies to practical applications. Most projects are carried out in partnership with the Illinois Department of Transportation (IDOT), with additional support from agencies such as the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA).

My research focuses on advanced numerical modeling, particularly FEM, to predict, analyze, and optimize the response of flexible pavement structures under complex loading conditions. My related work includes tire-pavement interaction modeling, mechanistic vehicle dynamics, and the use of machine learning (ML) techniques for high-dimensional engineering problems.
